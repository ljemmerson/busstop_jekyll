Volunteer with us

Become a volunteer with The Bus Stop and make a lasting difference to the lives of young people today.

  • Bus Drivers

    Volunteer drivers are invaluable to what we do! You don’t need any previous bus driving experience to volunteer as a driver - if you have a car licence and are over 25, then we can train you! Why not try something new?

  • Behind the Scenes

    There is a lot of upkeep that takes place with double decker buses! We are always in need of help to keep them on the road. This includes mechanics, general DIY and cleaning.

  • Youth Work Volunteers

    Could you help us with our day to day work with young people? This could be manning our cafe, playing games or mentoring a young person who’s going through a hard time. There are plenty of different ways you can help.
