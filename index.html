So how does it work?

We Come to You

Decked out with a café, consoles and craft areas, our mobile youth centre brings excellent youth work beyond the walls of your Church.

Reliable & Regular

Partnering with your church members, our skilled Youth Workers run weekly bespoke sessions that build lasting relationships with young people.

Trained Up

After working with your church, we leave your team fully skilled up and able to run their own Youth Groups.
