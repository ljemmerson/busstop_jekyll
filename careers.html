Work for us

Our trustees, youth workers and volunteers are passionate about seeing positive change in the lives of young people through the support of the local church.

  • Part-Time Youth Worker

    Salary: £18,000 pro rata.

    Hours: 20 hours per week - must be flexible with the days that you can work.

    Contract Length: This is a permanent post with 6 months probationary period.

    Annual Leave: 25 days + bank holidays pro rata.

    The Bus Stop is looking to appoint a part time Bus Youth Worker to work on the charity’s youth bus in North Yorkshire. The post will be based in Scarborough.

    An exciting opportunity to work in local communities on our state-of-the-art youth bus! We are looking for someone who is innovative, able to build relationships with all ages, keen to learn to drive a double decker bus (no prior experience necessary other than a valid driving license), experienced in leading youth work sessions and training and working with volunteers.

    To apply for this post, please send your CV and a cover letter to rosie@busstop.org.uk. In your cover letter, you should briefly explain why you believe you're the best candidate for the role. The closing date is the 11th July 2019 and interviews will be held in either York or Scarborough on the week beginning 21st July 2019.

    There is a genuine occupational requirement under the terms of the 2010 Equality Act that the postholder is a practising Christian.
